Medicinal Value Plants

Aloe Vera (கற்றாழை)

Medicinal Importance: Aloe Vera is known for its skin-healing properties, particularly for burns and cuts.
Habitat: Thrives in well-drained, sandy soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Basil (துளசி)

Medicinal Importance: Basil is an adaptogen, helping the body handle stress and promoting immune health.
Habitat: Prefers loamy soil and full sun, thriving in warm temperatures.

Mint (புத்தினா)

Medicinal Importance: Known for its digestive properties, mint also helps relieve headaches and nausea.
Habitat: Prefers moist, nutrient-rich soil and partial shade.

Rosemary (ரோஸ்மேரி)

Medicinal Importance: Rosemary is used for improving memory, reducing inflammation, and promoting circulation.
Habitat: Prefers well-draining, sandy soil and full sun exposure.

Lavender (லாவெண்டர்)

Medicinal Importance: Lavender is known for its calming properties, used to treat insomnia and anxiety.
Habitat: Thrives in dry, well-drained, sandy soils with full sun exposure.

Thyme (தைம்)

Medicinal Importance: Thyme is known for its antiseptic and antibacterial properties.
Habitat: Prefers well-draining, sandy soil and full sunlight.

Lemon Balm (எலுமிச்சை பாட்டு)

Medicinal Importance: Known for its calming and anti-stress properties, it also aids in digestion.
Habitat: Prefers well-draining soil and partial to full sunlight.

Fiddle Leaf Fig (பட்டு இலை பச்சை)

Medicinal Importance: While primarily ornamental, its leaves are used in traditional medicine for pain relief.
Habitat: Prefers well-draining loamy soil and bright, indirect light.

Terrace Garden Plants

Sunflower (சூரியகாந்தி)

How to Plant: Sunflowers thrive in full sun, so plant them in containers that receive at least 6-8 hours of direct sunlight daily.
Planting Tip: Use a well-draining potting mix and ensure regular watering, especially during dry periods.

Peace Lily (பீஸ் லிலி)

How to Plant: Ideal for shaded spots on the terrace. Plant in a medium-sized pot with rich, well-draining soil.
Planting Tip: Keep the soil consistently moist and avoid direct sunlight.

Tomato (தக்காளி)

How to Plant: Tomatoes need full sun, so place them in a sunny corner of the terrace. Plant in containers with a deep root system.
Planting Tip: Water consistently, ensuring good drainage to prevent root rot.

Ivy (அமைந்த கமல)

How to Plant: Ivy is great for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant along walls. Ensure the container has proper drainage.
Planting Tip: Ivy can grow well in partial sunlight and should be watered regularly but not overwatered.

Snake Plant (நாகமணிரொக்க)

How to Plant: This plant can be placed in a low-light area of your terrace. It grows well in pots with well-draining soil.
Planting Tip: Water sparingly, as it is drought-tolerant.

Bougainvillea (பூக்கொடி)

How to Plant: Bougainvillea requires full sun to bloom brightly. Plant it in a large container with a well-drained soil mix.
Planting Tip: Water only when the soil is dry to the touch to prevent root rot.
